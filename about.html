Our Mission

The Preston Consults Research and Data Analysis Team is committed to providing comprehensive, accurate, and actionable economic, trade, investment, financial and development data across Africa and beyond. We empower other units at Prestron Consults with the insights they need to facilitate and produce quality and evidence-base outputs.
